CSS Shaping Magic

This section merges the power of clip-path, mask-image, and shape-outside to produce professional, organic layouts. The top banner is clipped into a custom diagonal shape, the logo is revealed with a smooth vertical gradient mask, and the text wraps naturally around a circular floated image.

Such techniques are used in modern landing pages and magazine layouts for eye-catching asymmetry. They combine perfectly with transitions and responsive units for dynamic hero sections that truly stand out.
